Collage

  1. Each institution will be represented by one participant.
  2. Item will be conducted on the spot on the given topic / subject, sheet size 15” x22” .
  3. Duration will not be more than 2 hours 30 minutes.
  4. Participants shall bring their own scissors, pasting and other material required for the contest.
  5. Collage has to be prepared from old magazines. The organizers will provide the drawing paper of the size 15” x22”.
