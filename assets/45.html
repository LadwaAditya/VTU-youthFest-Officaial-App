Rangoli

  1. Each institution will be represented by one participant
  2. Duration will not be more than 2 hours 30 minutes
  3. Participants shall bring their own material. This art is known differently in various regions such as Mandna, Alpana, Alekhan, Kolam, Rangoli, etc. For this the medium and form for expression can be free hand, pictorial and descriptive. Only one medium shall be used – Poster Colors or Petals or Saw–dust or Pulses or Rice without pasting.
  4. The participants shall have to prepare a rangoli within the space provided by the organizer.
