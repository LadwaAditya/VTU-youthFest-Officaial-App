Debate

  1. Each institution will be represented by the two participants; one will speak FOR, while another will speak AGAINST the motion.
  2. Medium of the debate will be in Hindi or in English.
  3. Topics of the Debate will be announced 24 hours in advance.
  4. Each speaker will be allowed to speak for maximum five minutes.
  5. The competitor from each institution will speak FOR and AGAINST the motion. Paper reading is not allowed.
